Description

Five-spice powder is the key to this flavor-packed pork burger. A combination of star anise, cloves, cinnamon, fennel and Sichuan pepper, this rub is at once spicy, warm, sweet and (pleasingly) bitter, and has the power to put a tasty Asian spin on whatever you're cooking.
